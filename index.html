Olun's Valley

Introduction

Oluns is a 3 man grindspot located north of Odraxxia. It contains very few but powerful mobs. Recommended Ap and Dp is 300 and 400 but the local residents here grind with less than that.

Mobs

Olun's Golem

Its body consists of highly durable boulders covered in grass, as if grown during a long slumber. It boasts an enormous body with powerful movements. Its massive left arm is covered with Thornwood, and can kill intruders with a single blow.

Indomitable Golem

Its body consists of highly durable boulders covered in grass, as if grown during a long slumber. It's known to make downward attacks by utilizing its heavy weight and pointy torso.

Rock Golem

Its body consists of highly durable boulders covered in grass, as if grown during a long slumber. It's known to make downward attacks by utilizing its heavy weight and pointy shoulders.

Boulder Golem

Its body consists of highly durable boulders covered in grass, as if grown during a long slumber. Be wary of its unpredictable attacks as it can move regardless of its joints.

Attack Patterns

Chop

The golem raises its arm then slams it down. Duration: 9 seconds.

Stomp

The golem takes a step forward and stomps the ground. Duration: 7 seconds.

Slam

The golem raises both arms and slams in front of him. Duration: 7 seconds.

Swipe

The golem quickly swipes to his side. Duration: 6 seconds.

Breath

The golem sprays acid forwards. Duration: 10 seconds.

*This attack only occurs after engaging in combat for a long time, usually by slow clear speed.

Nuke

The golem takes aim at one player and slams his arm towards them, following out with a deadly shockwave.

Duration: 8 seconds.

*This attack only occurs when the targeted player is outside of the golem's attack range.

*Deals enough damage to almost or oneshot high end players.
